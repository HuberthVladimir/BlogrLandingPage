Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus one thing: creating contend. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, and Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy ways to add functionality or change the look of the blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through post. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality, you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and spedd in mind, worldwide data centers provide the backbone for ultra-fast connectivity. This ensures yout site will load instantly, no matter where your readers are, keeping your site competitive

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party, commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Anakytics. The architecture is clean and relatively easy to learn
